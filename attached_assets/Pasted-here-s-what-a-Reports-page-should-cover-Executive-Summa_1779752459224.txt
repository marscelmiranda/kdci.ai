here's what a Reports page should cover:

Executive Summary Report
A high-level snapshot clients can share internally — no technical detail, just outcomes. Fields to include: total tasks automated this period, time saved estimate, success rate, cost efficiency vs. manual work, and a one-paragraph AI-generated narrative summary.

Agent Activity Report
A breakdown of what the agents actually did. Fields: number of runs per agent, task categories handled, completion rate per agent, average duration, and most-used integrations/tools. Useful for clients who want to see ROI per workflow.

Chat Interaction Report
For clients using conversational AI. Fields: total sessions, average session length, most common topics or intents, escalation rate (how often a human had to step in), resolution rate, and CSAT or feedback scores if collected.

Error & Reliability Report
Builds trust by being transparent. Fields: total errors, error rate as a percentage, most common failure types in plain language, average time to resolution, and uptime percentage. Clients appreciate honesty here — it shows accountability.

Usage & Growth Report
Helps clients see adoption trends over time. Fields: month-over-month growth in tasks/sessions, user adoption across their team, peak usage periods, and plan utilization (how much of their quota they're using).

ROI Summary Report
The most compelling one for potential clients to share upward. Fields: hours saved (calculated from average task duration × runs), estimated dollar value saved (based on a configurable hourly rate), tasks that would have required manual effort, and a comparison to the previous period.

Report Generation Features to build in
Beyond the fields themselves, these are the features that make a Reports page feel polished for clients:

Date range selector — this period, last 30 days, custom range, or by quarter
Export options — PDF and CSV at minimum; Excel for more technical clients
Scheduled delivery — let clients set up automatic email delivery weekly or monthly
Branding — if this is white-labeled, allow a logo and company name on exported reports
Comparison toggle — show current vs. previous period side by side
AI-generated narrative — a 2–3 sentence plain-English summary auto-generated from the data, great for clients who need to present this to non-technical stakeholders
